Hackathon Projects

Tic Tac Time

Android application created at Hack the North. This app is designed to keep individuals from succumbing to the supposed luxury of the snooze button and simultaneously encouraging them to become intellectually ready to face the day by not allowing the app to stop ringing until after playing a tic tac toe game. I worked as the front-end developer on a team of three.

Fit to Fight

As a team of two, we created concept for web application Fit to Fight that has users donate $1 to local organizations for every 1,000 steps they are under their Fitbit step goal.

The app was created as part of a day long hackathon, ACApps, run by our local county to help solve poverty and hunger. We received Honorable Mention.
