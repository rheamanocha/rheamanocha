Side Projects

Fueled by pizza and passion

Filters For Me

I create custom Snapchat geofilters (location-based photo overlays) for parties, places, and more.
I worked on 30+ projects for a variety of clients. Stock photos courtesy of Unsplash.

Phototrace

Using Adobe Illustrator, I traced over a photo I had taken of Versailles. I digitally painted the phototrace with Adobe Photoshop. The goal was to realistically portray light and shadow while also emphasizing the contrast in the architectural elements.

Tic Tac Time

Android application created at Hack the North. Tic Tac Time is designed to keep individuals from succumbing to the supposed luxury of the snooze button by not allowing the app to stop ringing until they play a tic tac toe game. I worked as the front-end developer on a team of three.

Fit to Fight

During ACApps, a day-long event run by our local county to help solve poverty and hunger, we created a web app concept that has users donate $1 to local organizations for every 1,000 steps they are under their Fitbit step goal. We received Honorable Mention.
